Introduction

This is a portfolio site which consists of 5 different pages namely a home page, projects page, a video demonstration page, a contact page and finally here, a site report page. The knowledge and information gained from this module over the past few weeks were used to create this website from the basics of coding and web development all the way to design and debugging process.

Design Inspiration

I’ve chosen to go for the minimalistic yet sleek design of light green and grey, which looks appealing to the eyes (the hex codes were obtained from a Coolors Palette Generator). The font was a simple Arial with Sans Serif, taking insipration from my childhood word document reports. Taking inspiration from my module lecturer, Chris, I embraced the grid layout which he has presented in his lectures because “if it isn’t broke, don’t fix it” right?
